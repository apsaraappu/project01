There are 4 different combinations in css...!

  1. Descendent Selector(USING SPACE)
  2. Child Selector (USING > SYMBOLS)
  3. Adjacent Sibling Selector (USING + SYMBOLS)
  4. General Sibling Selector (USING ~ SYMBOL)

Descendent Selector(USING SPACE) Example....!

first H3 Tag....!(child)

First p Tag...!(child)

Second H3 Tag....!(child)

Second p Tag...!(child)

Third H3 Tag....!(Grand child)

Third p Tag...!(Grand child)

Fourth H3 Tag....!(child)

Fourth p Tag...!(child)
